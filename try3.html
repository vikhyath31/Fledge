This study investigates the power consumption of various home appliances and aims to provide insights into reducing energy consumption in households. The experiment involved measuring the power consumed by appliances such as refrigerators, washing machines, and televisions. The results show that these appliances consume a significant amount of energy, and the usage patterns of these appliances greatly affect their energy consumption.

About

Our app produces the rough idea about the power consumed by the household appliances at your houses.It provides information and resources to help households reduce their energy consumption and improve their energy efficiency.
